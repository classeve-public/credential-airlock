Migration & recovery

Recovery is a deliberate 2-of-3 ceremony. The vault key is split into three shares: this machine (DPAPI/OS), a recovery passphrase you choose, and an offline share you print and store. Any two can restore the vault on a new machine — no single share is enough.

Migration is already configured. Re-running would re-key the vault and invalidate the previous shares.

Write this down now. This offline share is shown once and is never stored or recoverable. Print it or copy it to a safe place. You will need it (plus either this machine or your passphrase) to recover the vault.
